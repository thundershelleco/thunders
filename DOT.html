About Us

Welcome to Daze of Thunders, the off-road racing
team fueled by passion, innovation, and the relentless
pursuit of excellence. Affiliated with the prestigious
University of Engineering and Technology (UET) Peshawar,
we are more than just a team we are a community of
trailblazers and thrill-seekers, united by our love
for off-road adventures.

The Daze of Thunders team is a dynamic and collaborative group of individuals who bring a diverse set of skills and perspectives to the table. United by a shared passion for innovation and excellence, our team thrives on the challenges that come with pushing the boundaries in our respective fields.

Events

In the rugged terrain of off-road racing, the Daze of Thunders off-road racing team emerged victorious in the highly anticipated SMME Piston Cup 2023. Fueled by a passion for speed and a commitment to excellence, the team navigated the challenging course with skill and precision, leaving a trail of dust in their wake. The thunderous roars of their powerful engines echoed through the hills as they conquered treacherous jumps, rocky stretches, and mud-laden trails. The Daze of Thunders showcased not only the prowess of their drivers but also the resilience of their meticulously tuned vehicles. With a harmonious blend of strategy and adrenaline-fueled action, the team's triumph in the SMME Piston Cup 2023 cemented their reputation as a force to be reckoned with in the exhilarating world of off-road racing.
